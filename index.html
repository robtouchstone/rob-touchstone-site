Meet Rob Touchstone

Certified CliftonStrengths Coach, Consultant & Keynote Speaker

As a Certified CliftonStrengths Coach, I have provided hundreds of hours of 1-on-1 coaching that includes students, faculty, business leaders, and couples to help them discover and leverage their unique strengths.


I’ve delivered numerous workshops for campuses, businesses, organizations, schools, and nonprofits. With my background in business, church leadership, and higher education as a professor, I'm also a frequent speaker and consultant with vast experience delivering keynotes, classes, lectures, sermons, devotional talks, and inspirational messages.


I'm happy to consider a wide variety of topics within my expertise to meet your needs. My areas of expertise include CliftonStrengths, entrepreneurship, startups, Business as Mission, business values, theology, pastoral ministry, vocational discovery, community development, creativity, and purpose. For over 10 years I have taught courses that include these areas and more.
